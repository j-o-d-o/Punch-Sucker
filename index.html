How do I controll this?
Two ways of controlling are supported. WebCam only works if your Browser supports WebRTC. You can switch the controlls by clicking middle Button in the top right corner of your screen.

Keyboard:
<
>
Move left / right with arrow keys
o
p
Punch left / right
WebCam:
Lean to the left to move left & Lean to the right to move right. Punch to the top right corner to throw a punch at a target with the right hand and to the top left corner for the left hand.
Try to position yourself that your shoulders are about at the half point of the webcam feed. You can test this by activing the "Debug-Layer" with the bottom button in the top right corner. The red square indicates the center of motion and determines where your game character is moving at. Some simple advice to get the best posible motion detection:

- Make sure the environment is illuminted well and evenly
- Position yourself as explained above with your shoulders being about at the vertical half
- Punch into the top left or right half of the webcam feed
